About Me

Hey! Welcome to my website. My name is Alex Obrads. I am an Astrophysics graduate currently working as a Data Engineer for a company called Quantexa.

This page could be considered a professional portfolio, but it also contains many of my personal projects.

I have quite a large range of interests, I enjoy problem solving, and I love to code. The result is that I have decent collection of DIY projects. I am currently working on building a trading bot, a media bias detector, improving some computational astrophysics code I wrote during uni, image processing tools for astrophotography, and this website!

The purpose of this page is to make my work accessible for those wanting to work on similar problems, and to connect with others that have similar projects or ideas.

Feel free to contact me if you want to chat! Thanks for stopping by :)
